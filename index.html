About AlexFilms

I’m Alexander Kagerer, the filmmaker behind AlexFilms, based in Málaga in southern Spain, working with couples who value a natural and cinematic approach. Rather than staged or overly directed footage, I focus on real interactions, subtle moments and the overall atmosphere of the day. Each film is edited with careful attention to pacing, music and sound, creating something that feels immersive and personal rather than traditional. Alongside ground cinematography, I offer aerial coverage with professional drones and optional FPV footage — adding a wider visual perspective, especially suited for coastal venues, villas and destination weddings.
