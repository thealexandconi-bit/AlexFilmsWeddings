About AlexFilms

My name is Alexander Kagerer, a 32-year-old professional wedding videographer based in Málaga, specializing in cinematic storytelling and high-end wedding films. Using a Netflix-certified cinema camera, a professional drone, and my expertise in editing, color grading, and sound design, I craft each film with great attention to detail. My goal is to capture genuine emotions, natural moments, and a distinctive cinematic look that reflects each couple’s unique story. Working across the Costa del Sol—including Málaga, Marbella, and destinations throughout Spain and Europe—is truly a dream come true. What brings me the greatest satisfaction is seeing the joy and emotional reactions of couples when they receive their wedding films—timeless memories they can cherish for a lifetime.
